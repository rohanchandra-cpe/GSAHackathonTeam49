Food safety refers to the conditions and practices that preserve the quality of food to prevent contamination and food-borne illnesses. The Food Safety and Inspection Service of the United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) educates consumers about the importance of safe food handling and how to reduce the risks associated with foodborne illness. Call the USDA Meat and Poultry Hotline at 1-888-MPHotline (1-888-674-6854) to get information on safe food storage, handling and preparation.
